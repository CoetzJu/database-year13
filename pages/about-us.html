About Us

This website was developed by one person who wanted to create a website that will help get young and old people to get interested in the idea of using Micro:bits and programming. The reason why we chose to use micro:bits as the introduction to programming is because the micro:bits were designed by the BBC for the education of beginners and young school students. Please bare in mind we do not own the micro:bit product and only provide information about them.
